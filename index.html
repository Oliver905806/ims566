UiTM Komander Management System

U-KOMS is an integrated online platform specifically designed to manage and streamline the administrative and operational aspects of the UiTM Commander Program.

Profile Management

By digitizing records and streamlining administrative tasks, U-KOMS aims to foster productivity and provide easy access to essential information for all stakeholders, including students, staff, and administrators.

The system provides a centralized hub for managing e-profiles, enhancing communication, and ensuring the efficient organization of activities and resources for commanders.
